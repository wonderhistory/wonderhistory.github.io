About Wonder History:

Wonder History is an online organisation tht provides free and easy to use resourses such as colourful Flash cards, nicely laid out and easy to read notes plus lots more. Our aim is to provide a free educational network that kids can easily learn History, while educators can teach it. We currently are woking on the Junior Cert History, but we hope to add Leaving Cert to our website soon. If your school is interested in using our resourses, please contact us at wonderhistoryproject@gmail. We hope you enjoy and learn lots from our website.

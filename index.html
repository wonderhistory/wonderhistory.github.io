Welcome to Wonder History!

A free and fully responsive website that helps you study History!

Wonder History aims to help you and your class mates study History, We offer many courses that include simple notes and colourful online flashcards. All our courses are 100% free and you don't need an account to view them. They are a massive support to not only students but also teachers. All of our courses can be views from the navigation bar to your left!

What we offer:

Free resourses

We offer 100% free resources to help your or your class learn History on the go and in class!

Fun and easy to use

All of our History resourse are fun and easy to use, they are all very colourful and can be accessed at a click of a button.

Many different resourses:

We offer many differnt resources, not only do we provide free notes and flashcards we also provide project help and an email you can ask any History question too!

All non profit

We make 100% no profit on this website!
